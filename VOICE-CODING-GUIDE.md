   - Is mic plugged in?
   - Is mic muted?
   - Test in browser settings

   - Speak clearly
   - Reduce background noise
   - Lower VoiceConfig.minConfidence
   - Check selected language matches your accent

1. **Make Sure You're Awake**

   - If not working, try: "command save file"
